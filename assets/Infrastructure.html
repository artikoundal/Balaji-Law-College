Infrastructure

  1. Home / Infrastructure
  2. Infrastructure

A state-of art Moot Court Hall serves as an experiential learning place. The moot court room is built separately to conduct the internal moots as well as for hosting the moot court competition. The feel of a classic court room is simulated through this Moot Court Hall.

Balaji Law College has a seminar hall with well-equipped Information Technology to conduct academic discussions, deliberations, and workshops.

The library has a well-equipped computer facility with online legal databases like Manupatra (national and international), EBSCO, and a free internet facility. The library consists of a total of 16926 books, which include 908 reference books, 2475 general books, 14 journals, 10 newspapers, and 1 computer lab consisting of 10 computers. The students are allowed to draw 10 books at a time for a period of 30 days (Can Re-issue the book after 30 days). The library subscribes to all leading newspapers, periodicals, and all national and international magazines. Students, faculty members, and scholars are encouraged to recommend as many books of international standards as are required by them. The library is open to students during college hours. The timing for issuing the books is between 08:00 a.m. and 4:00 p.m. We also have Central Library of Sri Balaji Society, Pune which contains all international books, reference books related to the courses, management, and international scenario with spacious and well- furnished reading hall.
