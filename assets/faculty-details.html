Mrs. Kalyani Sharad Kakade

(B.S.L.LLB, LL.M, NET)

Biography

Mrs. Kalyani Sharad Kakade completed her B.S.LL.B from Dr. D.Y.Patil Law College, Pune. LL.M (International Law) from Law Department, Savitribai Phule Pune University. She has qualified for the UGC NET Examination 2018 and has an expertise in a variety of academic subjects encompassing Contact Law, TOPA, CPC, CrPC Social Research Methodology and Comparative Criminal Justice System. She has prior teaching experience in SNBP Law College, Pune and Priyadarshini Law College, Satara. She joined BLC in March 2022,
