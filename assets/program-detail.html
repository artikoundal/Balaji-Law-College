The 5-year B.A.LL.B is a full-time, Integrated Law Course, accredited by The Bar Council of India. The BA LLB syllabus includes legal and non-legal subjects. The first 4 semesters are dominated by an interdisciplinary study of Arts stream with legal subjects and the rest 6 semesters consist of law subjects. This course is structured with a balanced combination of classroom learning as well as mock court drills, research work, case studies, law internships, etc. Admission to the B.A. LL.B. (Hons.) degree programme is purely on the basis of merit as assessed through a MAH-L.L.B.5Yrs.(Five-Year Integrated Course)-CET conducted for the purpose.

  • Academic qualification - Higher Secondary School Certificate Examination (10+2) or its equivalent with 45% marks. There shall be relaxation of 5% marks in case of SC/ST students within the state of Maharashtra. Non-Zero Score in MAH-LLB-5 CET will not be eligible for the admission.
  • Applicants who have obtained 10+2 higher secondary pass certificate or first-degree certificate after prosecuting studies in distance or correspondence method are eligible for admission in the integrated five-year course.
  • Applicants who have obtained 10+2 or graduation / post- graduation through open universities system, directly without having any basic qualification for prosecuting such studies are NOT ELIGIBLE for admission in law course.
