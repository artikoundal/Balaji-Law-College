About Us

Inspiring Legal Minds
Transforming Justice

Balaji Law College, Pune, established in 2003, is affiliated with Savitribai Phule Pune University and recognized by the Government of Maharashtra and the Bar Council of India. The college operates under the aegis of Sri Balaji Society, Pune, and upholds the Society’s core values of Discipline, Dedication, and Determination. It aims to meet the evolving needs of society by preparing skilled Practicing and Corporate Lawyers.

Founder's Message

Sri Balaji Society, Pune means quality and commitment to the students’ community. We believe that each and every student has the potential to be groomed to have a successful corporate career. Converting the right candidates to the right jobs through right training and development has always been the goal of our Society. We are deeply pleased to state that we have achieved the same to a larger extent as can be seen from the Track-Record of campus placements of our management institutes.

Late Prof. Dr. (Col.) A. Balasubramanian

our principal

Principal's Message

I heartily welcome you all at Balaji Law College, the destination of opportunities. BLC is blessed with the visionary founder Late Col. Prof. Bala Sir. The principles of Discipline, Determination & Dedication forms the foundation of Law College.

Our college believes in participative teaching learning culture and we are committed to build a student centric experience-based learning system. College has a well-structured & robust academic orientation and we offer a Five years integrated course & Three years LL.B course. We also offer Diploma in Taxation Law to our students

Dr. Tejaswi R. Avhad
B.S.L., L.L.B., L.L.M., SET, PH.D - Principal
